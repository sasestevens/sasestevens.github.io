Hey folks, welcome back to Stevens. You're receiving this email because you expressed interest in attending the SASE National Conference. The eboard has been hard at work figuring out the finances and planning for our chapter's attendance at NC. Everything you need to know is below:

  • Students will be responsible for the cost of registration (approx. $90) and the flight (approx. $200-350).
  • SASE Stevens will be covering the hotel and may reimburse each student for some amount of the registration (depending on remaining funds).
  • Email sase@stevens.edu to confirm you are attending before you register or pay for anything.
  • Once SASE Stevens responds and confirms you are cleared to register, you may go proceed to register and book your arrangements.
  • SASE Stevens will contact you to confirm your registration. We will also need your contact email, phone number, and estimated time/date of arrival in Houston.
  • You will be required to attend upcoming information sessions and fill out forms for Stevens on DuckSync (both to be announced soon).

In addition, the upcoming deadline for reduced registration costs is September 12th. After the deadline, the registration cost will increase to $110. Booking a flight ahead of time will likely be significantly less expensive as well.
